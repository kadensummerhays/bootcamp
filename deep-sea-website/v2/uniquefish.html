Midnight Zone: There is a fish that has a little lure on top of its head. This lure has a light that attracts squids and other fish. This fish is called the Angler Fish. He lives in what is called the midnight zone. Another word for this is dark places.

Food: Angler Fish hunt small squids and eat fish that get attracted by their lure.

Their Name: Porcupine Puffer fish get their name because they suck in lots of water that makes them blow up three times bigger than their usual body and they have lots of spikes, like a porcupine, on their body. The spikes on their body are also poisonous as a defense mechanism.

Food: Puffer Fish eat crab, plankten, and other small sea creatures.

Looks: Groopers often are brown. Their teeth point up so that they can hold onto their food.

Looks: These look like your ordinary fish, but they are brown that have stripes on their back.

Food: Triggerfish eat algae and seaweed.

Defense: In order to save themselves from their vulturous enemies (also known as sharks), they have a good defense machanism; camouflage.

Looks: Frogfish look very unique. These fish walk on their back fins in order to blend in with the coral.

Food: Frogfish can eat fish bigger than them (except for the ones that actually eat them). They lure their prey with a lure they have attached to them which looks like a fake shrimp. They it comes and the frogfish attacks! They attack in 6 ms.
